FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:
Q: Do I need to book an appointment or can I walk in?
A: We recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting, but walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

Q: How long does a routine checkup take?
A: A routine checkup typically takes 20 to 30 minutes.

Q: Is teeth cleaning painful?
A: Teeth cleaning is generally painless. You may feel slight sensitivity but it's very manageable.

Q: How often should I get a dental checkup?
A: We recommend a dental checkup every 6 months for optimal oral health.

Q: Do you offer EMI or payment plans?
A: Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for treatments above ₹10,000. Please ask at the reception.

Q: What should I do in a dental emergency?
A: Call us immediately. We handle emergency cases on priority. If it's after hours, leave a message and we'll get back to you.

Q: How long does a root canal take?
A: A root canal usually takes 1 to 2 sessions, each lasting about 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Q: What should I bring to my first appointment?
A: Please bring any previous dental records, X-rays, and a valid ID. Arrive 10 minutes early to fill out forms.

APPOINTMENT POLICIES:
- Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time.
- Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance.
- No-shows may be charged a nominal fee.
- Emergency cases are always prioritized.

POST-TREATMENT CARE TIPS:
- After extraction: Avoid hot food/drinks for 24 hours. Use ice pack for swelling.
- After filling: Avoid chewing on that side for 2-3 hours until numbness wears off.
- After cleaning: Mild sensitivity is normal for 1-2 days.
- After root canal: Avoid hard foods until the permanent crown is placed.
